Rules

What is Golf?

The objective of golf is to hit the ball in the hole in the least amount of strokes. There are 18 holes on a given course. A player drives the ball of the tee box towards the green in hopes of landing in the fairway. Hitting towards the flag is the goal trying to avoid any water or sand bunkers.

Scoring

Par on a hole is the number of strokes a skilled golfer is expected to need to complete the hole. The par is primarilly determined by the distance to the hole with hazards and other geographical features playing less of a factor. The minimum par for any hole is three strokes, and most holes have a par of 3, 4, or 5 making the overall par for a course around 70 to 72. A players score is compared to the overall par score.
