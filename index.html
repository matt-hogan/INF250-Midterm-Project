The History of Golf

The modern game of golf originated in Scotland in the early 1500s. In 1764, the standard 18-hole golf course was created at the Old Course at St. Andrews in Scotland. The earliest rules were created in 1744, and most of them are still used today.

Golf Today

Today, golf has expanded all across the world. In the United States, there are 16,693 golf course with about 25 million people, or 8 % of the populaiton, playing golf. Golf is becoming more popular every year especially to younger players.
